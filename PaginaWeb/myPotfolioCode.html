PROJECT KATHARSIS

DESIGNER - PROGRAMMER

The love child of an RTS, a Survival game and an Adventure game, Katharsis places the player in the role of A.R.K.A, an emergency AI tasked with the mission of protecting a group of survivors on a mysterious alien planet.

Explore the 3D Low Poly planet K32-Aplha and assign work tasks to your people. Gain resources to craft tools and gear while unraveling the secrets of a long civilization and the reason it is gone. Be quick though because at night it is too dangerous to walk about. It is then that the player must prepare and distribute rations, establish guard duties and plan future expeditions.
